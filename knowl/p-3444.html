Consider an arbitrary positive function \(f\) on \([a,b]\) with the interval subdivided as shown in Figure 4.2.3, and choose to use left endpoints. Then on each interval \([x_{i}, x_{i+1}]\text{,}\) the area of the rectangle formed is given by

\begin{equation*} A_{i+1} = f(x_i) \cdot \Delta x\text{,} \end{equation*}

as seen in Figure 4.2.4.
