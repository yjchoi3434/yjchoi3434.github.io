Looking at the composite functions that are present, there are two possible \(u\)-substitutions to try. One is \(u = 1+x^2\text{,}\) for which \(du = 2x \, dx\) and \(x = \sqrt{u-1}\text{.}\) Because there is no \(x \, dx\) term present in the integral, this substitution will not work.
