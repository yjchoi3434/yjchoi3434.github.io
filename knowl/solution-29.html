Solution 1.3.3.2.1.
\(B=(3.2,4.4)\) is on the graph of \(g\text{,}\) we have \(g(3.2)=4.4\text{.}\) The slope of the tangent line touching the graph at \(x=2\) is given by
\begin{equation*} \mbox{slope} = \frac{\mbox{rise}}{\mbox{run}} = \frac{4.4-4.33}{3.2-3.18} = 3.5. \end{equation*}
Thus, \(g'(3.2)=3.5\)
