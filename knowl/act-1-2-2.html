Activity 1.2.3.

Consider a moving object whose position function is given by \(s(t) = t^2\text{,}\) where \(s\) is measured in meters and \(t\) is measured in minutes.

  1. Determine the most simplified expression for the average velocity of the object on the interval \([3, 3+h]\text{,}\) where \(h \gt 0\text{.}\)

  2. Determine the average velocity of the object on the interval \([3,3.2]\text{.}\) Include units on your answer.

  3. Determine the instantaneous velocity of the object when \(t = 3\text{.}\) Include units on your answer.
