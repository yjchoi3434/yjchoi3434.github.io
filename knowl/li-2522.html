Item d.

We know \(v_{\text{AVG} }[a,b] = \frac{1}{b-a} \int_a^b v(t) \, dt\text{,}\) so

\begin{equation*} v_{\text{AVG} }[0,4] = \frac{1}{4-0} \int_0^4 \sqrt{4-(t-2)^2} \, dt = \frac{1}{4} 2\pi = \frac{\pi}{2}\text{,} \end{equation*}

which is measured in meters per minute, since the units on “4” are minutes and on “\(2\pi\)” are meters.
