Item 8.3.7.8.b.i.

For any \(k\) we have \(k \gt k-1\text{,}\) so if \(k \gt 1\) then it follows that \(0 \lt \frac{1}{k} \lt \frac{1}{k-1}\text{.}\) Since the harmonic series \(\sum \frac{1}{k}\) diverges, the Direct Comparison Test shows that the larger series \(\sum \frac{1}{k-1}\) must also diverge.
