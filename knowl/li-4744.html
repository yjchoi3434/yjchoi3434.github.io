Item 8.2.3.9.c.

We know \(P_2 = \left(1 + \frac{r}{12}\right)P_1 - M\text{,}\) so we substitute for \(P_1\) and simplify:

\begin{align*} P_2 \amp = \left(1 + \frac{r}{12}\right)P_1 - M\\ \amp = \left(1 + \frac{r}{12}\right)\left[\left(1+\frac{r}{12}\right)P - M\right] - M\\ \amp = \left(1 + \frac{r}{12}\right)^2P - \left[1 + \left(1+\frac{r}{12}\right)\right] M\text{.} \end{align*}
