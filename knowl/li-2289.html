Item c.

By calculating the area bounded by the curve, we find 1 unit of area on \([0,1]\text{,}\) 4 units of area on \([1,4]\text{,}\) and 8 units of area on \([4,8]\text{,}\) thus the total distance traveled on \(0 \le t \le 8\) is \(D = 1 + 4 + 8\) meters. As the change in position is given by the net signed area on this interval, we find that the change in position is

\begin{equation*} s(8) - s(0) = 1 - 4 + 8 = 5 \ \mbox{m}\text{.} \end{equation*}

We thus observe that the distance traveled and change in position on \([0,8]\) are different.
