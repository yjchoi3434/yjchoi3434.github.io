Item b.i.

With the cone having its base centered at the origin and being a solid of revolution about the \(x\)-axis, the cone is generated by the line \(y = 4 - \frac{4}{5}x\text{.}\) Using the disk method, it follows that the cone's volume is

\begin{equation*} V = \int_{0}^{5} \pi (4 - \frac{4}{5}x)^2 \, dx = \frac{80\pi}{3} \approx 83.7758 \mbox{m}^3\text{.} \end{equation*}
