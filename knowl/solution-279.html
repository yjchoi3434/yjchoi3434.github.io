Solution 4.4.5.4.1.

Since the function \(f(x)=64 - x^2\) is even,

\begin{equation*} \int_{-8}^{8} (64 -x^2)\, dx = 2 \int_{0}^{8} (64 -x^2)\, dx = 2 \left. \left(64 x - \frac{x^3}{3}\right) \right|_ {0}^{8} = 2 \left[ \left(64 \cdot 8- \frac{8^3}{3}\right) - (0-0) \right] = 2 \frac{1024}{3} = 682.666666666667\text{.} \end{equation*}
