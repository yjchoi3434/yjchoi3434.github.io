Item 7.5.3.9.c.

Letting \(S(t)\) denotes the number of grams of salt in the tank at time \(t\text{,}\) since there are \(100\) gallons of solution in the tank at any time \(t\text{,}\) the concentration of salt in the tank in grams per gallon is

\begin{equation*} \frac{S(t)}{100} \frac{\text{grams}}{\text{gallon}} \end{equation*}

at any time \(t\text{.}\)
