Item 7.5.3.8.c.

While the function \(w(t) = \sqrt{7t+64}\) grows without bound as \(t\) increases, the model is unrealistic more because the function initially doesn't grow fast enough. For example, the model predicts that at age \(10\text{,}\) \(w(120) \approx 30.06\) pounds, and at age \(20\text{,}\) \(w(120) = 41.76\) pounds. A realistic model would need to grow much more quickly early in life and then level off around age \(20\text{.}\)
