For the total distance traveled, \(D\text{,}\) we take the opposite of the change in position on any interval where the velocity is negative. Hence,

\begin{align*} D &= \int_0^{0.5} v(t) dt - \int_{0.5}^{3.5} v(t) dt + \int_{3.5}^{4} v(t) dt\\ &= 0.25 + 3 + 0.125\\ &= 3.375 \end{align*}
