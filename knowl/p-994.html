These results show from a different perspective that \(g'(0)\) does not exist. For small negative \(h\) values, the difference quotient is negative and getting more negative without bound. For small positive values of \(h\text{,}\) the difference quotient is positive and increasing without bound. This shows that \(\lim_{h \to 0} \frac{\sqrt{|h|}}{h}\) does not exist.
