Solution 1.1.4.1.1.
\(0 \le t \le 0.2\) is given by \(\frac{s(0.2)-s(0)}{0.2}=\frac{0.3}{0.2}=1.5\) ft/s. The average velocity over the interval \(0.2 \le t \le 0.4\) is given by \(\frac{s(0.4)-s(0.2)}{0.2}=\frac{1.7-0.3}{0.2}=7\) ft/s. A reasonable estimate for the velocity at \(t=0.2\) is the average \(\frac{1}{2}\cdot (1.5+7) = 4.25\)
